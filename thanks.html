the Process...

Order Request Sent

You have just submitted your order form with character details.

Review

The artist has reviewed your form, and will reach out to you shortly if they have any further questions. An invoice will also be sent out.

Concept Sketch

The artist will create a rough concept sketch for you to review. Please raise any concerns you may have. This initial sketch is to give you a rough idea of the general posing, expressions, character design.

Line Work

The artist has translated the concept sketch into line art. This will be your final opportunity to review the piece before the artist moves forward.

Final Rendering

The artist has now coloured, shaded, and added any final details.

Delivery

Keep an eye on your inbox! The artist will send you the final high res file of your illustration, along with any extras you may have ordered.
